Bernard Arnault #3

2022 Billionaires Net Worth : $158 B

Bernard Arnault is a French billionaire businessman and art collector. He is the Chairman and CEO of LVMH Moët Hennessy – Louis Vuitton SE, the world's largest luxury goods company.

Under Arnault's leadership, LVMH has grown to become a global empire, with over 70 luxury brands, including Louis Vuitton, Dior, Fendi, and Givenchy, among others. Arnault is known for his astute business acumen, his focus on innovation and creativity, and his passion for art and design.

Arnault started his career in the construction industry and later moved into the world of luxury goods. He has been instrumental in turning around several struggling luxury brands and has been a key driver of LVMH's growth and success.

In addition to his business activities, Arnault is also a prominent philanthropist and art collector. He has donated millions of dollars to various charitable causes and has amassed an impressive art collection, which includes works by Pablo Picasso, Claude Monet, and Andy Warhol, among others.
