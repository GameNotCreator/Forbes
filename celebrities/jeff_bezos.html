Jeff Bezos #2

2022 Billionaires Net Worth : $171 B

Jeff Bezos is a billionaire entrepreneur and the founder and former CEO of Amazon, one of the world's largest online retailers. Bezos is known for his innovative approach to business, his customer-centric philosophy, and his ability to disrupt traditional industries.

Under Bezos' leadership, Amazon has expanded into multiple business areas, such as cloud computing, streaming services, and artificial intelligence. The company has also become a major player in the retail industry, disrupting brick-and-mortar stores and traditional supply chains.

Bezos is also the owner of The Washington Post and Blue Origin, a space exploration company. He has been involved in several philanthropic efforts, such as the Bezos Day One Fund, which focuses on supporting homeless families and creating high-quality, full-scholarship preschools.

Bezos is known for his relentless focus on long-term growth and his willingness to take risks. He has been recognized for his contributions to business and technology, receiving several awards and honors, including the Buzz Aldrin Space Innovation Award and the Axel Springer Award for his visionary entrepreneurship.
