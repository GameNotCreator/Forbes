Larry Page #6

2022 Billionaires Net Worth : $111 B

Larry Page is a billionaire entrepreneur and computer scientist, best known as the co-founder of Google, one of the world's most valuable and innovative companies. Along with his partner, Sergey Brin, Page revolutionized the way people access and use information on the internet, creating a powerful search engine that has become a ubiquitous tool for billions of people worldwide.

Beyond his contributions to the tech industry, Page has been involved in several philanthropic endeavors, particularly in the areas of healthcare and renewable energy. He has also invested in several startups and companies, such as SpaceX and Planetary Resources, that are focused on space exploration and colonization.

Page holds a degree in computer science from Stanford University and has been recognized for his contributions to technology and entrepreneurship, receiving numerous awards and honors, including the Marconi Prize and the Carnegie Mellon University School of Computer Science Distinguished Alumni Award.
