Mukesh Ambani #10

2022 Billionaires Net Worth: $90.7 B

Mukesh Ambani is an Indian billionaire businessman and the chairman and largest shareholder of Reliance Industries, a conglomerate with interests in petrochemicals, refining, oil, and gas exploration. He is also involved in the telecommunications industry with his company Reliance Jio, which has become one of the largest mobile network operators in India.

Ambani is known for his ambitious plans to transform India's economy through the use of technology and for his strong advocacy of the digital revolution in India. He has been recognized for his contributions to the Indian economy and society, receiving numerous awards and honors.

Ambani is also involved in philanthropy through the Reliance Foundation, which supports various causes such as education, healthcare, and rural development in India.
