Steve Ballmer #10

2022 Billionaires Net Worth : $91.4 B

Steve Ballmer is an American businessman and investor who is best known for his tenure as CEO of Microsoft from 2000 to 2014. During his time at Microsoft, Ballmer oversaw the development of some of the company's most successful products, including Windows XP, Xbox, and the Microsoft Office suite.

After leaving Microsoft, Ballmer became the owner of the Los Angeles Clippers, a professional basketball team in the NBA. He has also been involved in several philanthropic ventures, including the Ballmer Group, which focuses on improving economic mobility for children and families in the United States.

Ballmer is known for his energetic and passionate leadership style, as well as his competitive spirit. He is a strong advocate for technology and its potential to drive innovation and growth in the global economy.

Ballmer holds a degree in applied mathematics and economics from Harvard University, where he was also a member of the varsity basketball team. He has been recognized for his contributions to business and philanthropy, receiving numerous awards and honors throughout his career.
