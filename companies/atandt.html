AT&T #5

2022 Company Net Worth: $228.2 billion

AT&T is a telecommunications company that provides wireless, broadband, and entertainment services to consumers and businesses. Founded in 1983, AT&T has grown to become one of the largest telecommunications companies in the world, with operations in more than 200 countries.

AT&T's product line includes wireless devices and services, high-speed internet, digital TV, and home phone. The company has also been involved in developing new technologies such as 5G networks and edge computing.

AT&T is known for its extensive network infrastructure, which includes cell towers, fiber-optic cables, and data centers. The company has faced criticism over its pricing policies and its role in the debate over net neutrality.

Despite these challenges, AT&T remains a major player in the telecommunications industry and continues to invest in new technologies and services to stay competitive.
