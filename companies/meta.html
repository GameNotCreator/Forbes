Meta #10

2022 Company Net Worth: $1.05 trillion

Meta, formerly known as Facebook, is a social media and technology company that was founded in 2004. The company operates several social networking platforms, including Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger, which have a combined user base of billions of people worldwide.

In addition to social networking, Meta is also involved in various other technology fields, such as virtual reality, artificial intelligence, and e-commerce. The company's long-term vision is to create a metaverse, a virtual world where people can interact with each other and digital content in immersive ways.

Meta has faced numerous controversies over the years, including issues with user data privacy, content moderation, and election interference. The company has made efforts to address these concerns through various initiatives and policy changes.

Despite these challenges, Meta remains one of the most valuable and influential companies in the world, with a significant impact on the way people connect and communicate with each other.
