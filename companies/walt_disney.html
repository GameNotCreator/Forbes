Walt Disney #9

2022 Company Net Worth: $192 billion

Walt Disney is a multinational media and entertainment company that was founded in 1923 by Walt Disney and Roy O. Disney. The company is known for producing and distributing a wide range of content, including movies, television shows, and theme park attractions.

Some of the most well-known brands and franchises owned by Disney include Marvel, Star Wars, Pixar, and ESPN. Disney is also the parent company of the ABC television network.

The company has a strong reputation for storytelling and creativity, and has won numerous awards for its films and television shows. Disney has also made significant investments in technology, particularly in the areas of streaming and digital content distribution.

Despite its success, Disney has faced some challenges in recent years, particularly due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The company was forced to close its theme parks and delay the release of several highly anticipated films.

Nevertheless, Disney remains one of the most valuable media companies in the world, with a vast portfolio of beloved brands and franchises.
