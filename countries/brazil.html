Brazil #9

2021 GDP: $2.77 trillion 2021 GDP per capita : $13,235

Brazil is the largest country in South America and the fifth largest country in the world by both land area and population. The country is home to diverse landscapes, including the Amazon rainforest, beaches, and bustling cities like São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro.

Brazil is a mixed economy and is one of the world's largest agricultural producers and exporters. The country has a growing manufacturing industry and is also known for its natural resources, including oil and minerals.

Brazil is a member of the BRICS group of emerging economies and is also a member of the G20. The country is working to strengthen its role in global affairs and has been actively seeking to increase its presence in international organizations.

Despite facing challenges such as income inequality and crime, Brazil has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its music, dance, and sports, including soccer and capoeira.
