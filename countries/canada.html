Canada #10

2021 GDP: $1.84 trillion 2021 GDP per capita: $48,377

Canada is a North American country known for its vast and diverse landscape, ranging from the Rocky Mountains to its picturesque coastlines. With a population of over 38 million people, Canada is a multicultural nation that values diversity and inclusivity.

Canada is a member of the G7 and has the 10th largest economy in the world by nominal GDP. The country is known for its abundance of natural resources, including oil, gas, and minerals. Canada also has a highly skilled and educated workforce, with a focus on innovation and technology.

In addition to its economic prowess, Canada is recognized for its universal healthcare system, strong social safety net, and commitment to environmental sustainability. The country is also active in international affairs, participating in various global organizations such as the United Nations and NATO.
