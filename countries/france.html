France #6

2021 GDP: $3.15 trillion 2021 GDP per capita: $47,488

France is a Western European country famous for its rich history, culture, and cuisine. With a population of over 67 million, France is the world's 7th largest economy by nominal GDP and a member of the G7 group of leading industrialized countries.

France is renowned for its fashion industry, art, and architecture. It is home to some of the world's most famous landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and the Palace of Versailles.

France's economy is highly developed and diversified, with key sectors including aerospace, automobile manufacturing, and luxury goods. The country is also a leading exporter of agricultural products such as wine, cheese, and meat.

France is a founding member of the European Union and plays an active role in global affairs. The country has a highly educated workforce and is committed to promoting innovation and sustainability.
