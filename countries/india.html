India #7

2021 GDP: $3.06 trillion 2021 GDP per capita: $2,206

India is a diverse country located in South Asia, with a population of over 1.3 billion people. It is known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and breathtaking natural beauty. The country is home to many major cities, including Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore, and is rapidly developing into a global economic powerhouse.

India's mixed economy is characterized by a large public sector and a growing private sector, which has made it one of the fastest-growing major economies in the world. The country is a leader in industries such as information technology, pharmaceuticals, and automotive manufacturing. Additionally, India has a highly skilled and educated workforce, with many workers fluent in English, which has made it a hub for outsourcing and offshoring.

Beyond its economic might, India is also a key player in global politics, as evidenced by its membership in the United Nations, G20, and other international organizations. The country is also a nuclear power and has the fourth-largest military in the world. India is known for its rich cultural heritage and has made significant contributions to fields such as mathematics, science, and literature.
