United Kingdom #5

2021 GDP : $2.85 trillion 2021 GDP per capita : $42,619

The United Kingdom, located in Europe, is a country consisting of England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. With a population of over 67 million people, the UK is known for its rich history, culture, and diverse landscape, which includes bustling cities like London, Edinburgh, and Cardiff, as well as stunning natural scenery in places like the Lake District and the Scottish Highlands.

The UK has a mixed economy with a significant private sector and a highly skilled workforce. It is a leader in industries like finance, technology, and manufacturing. The country is also known for its cultural exports, such as music, film, and literature, which have had a significant impact on global culture.

The United Kingdom is a key player in global politics, with a permanent seat on the UN Security Council and membership in international organizations like NATO and the EU. Its military is one of the most advanced and well-equipped in the world and participates in various international missions. The UK is also known for its contributions to science, medicine, and other fields of research.

The country is currently undergoing a period of significant change as it prepares to leave the European Union and forge new relationships with other countries around the world.
